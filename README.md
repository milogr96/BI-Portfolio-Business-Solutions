# BI Portfolio: Business Solutions 📊

Welcome! This repository showcases a series of Business Intelligence projects designed to solve real-world challenges across various business units. Each dashboard focuses on **data integrity, advanced DAX modeling, and storytelling**.


## 📈 1. Sales Performance & Revenue Tracking
**Focus:** Commercial Strategy & Growth
* **Objective:** Monitor sales trends, YTD (Year-to-Date) growth, and regional performance.
* **Key Features:** Dynamic filters for product categories, time-intelligence DAX measures, and revenue forecasting.
* **Impact:** Enables leadership to identify underperforming regions and optimize commercial efforts.

## 👥 2. Human Resources (People Analytics)
**Focus:** Operational Efficiency & Talent Management
* **Objective:** Analyze headcount distribution, turnover rates, and employee demographics.
* **Key Features:** Organizational structure visualizations and attrition rate analysis using Power Query for data cleaning.
* **Impact:** Provides HR managers with a clear view of workforce stability and recruitment needs.

## 🎧 3. Customer Service Operations
**Focus:** Quality Assurance & Process Optimization
* **Objective:** Track support ticket volume, agent performance, and customer satisfaction (CSAT) scores.
* **Key Features:** Response time distribution and SLA (Service Level Agreement) compliance monitoring.
* **Impact:** Identifies bottlenecks in support workflows to improve the overall customer experience.


## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills
* **Data Transformation:** Power Query (M Language).
* **Data Modeling:** Star Schema, DAX (Calculated Measures & Columns).
* **Visualization:** Power BI Desktop (Bookmarks, Tooltips, Drill-through).
